About Superior Pool Service

Superior Pool Service Inc. has been located in East Bridgewater since 1978. We are proud to be one of the longest-standing family pool companies on the South Shore.

We are associated with the American Swimming Pool Association (ASPA) and the BBB. We carry premium brands like Hayward Pool Products, Latham Pool, and Polaris Pool.

Frequently Asked Questions

Are you licensed and insured?

Yes, Superior Pool Service is both licensed and insured. When you choose us, you can rest assured that your investment is protected.

How many years of experience do you have?

Our team has over 45 years of experience in the pool and hot tub industry.

What type of pools do you install?

We strictly install inground, vinyl-liner pools. As a specialized swimming pool contractor, we focus on delivering high-quality, long-lasting vinyl pool installations.

Do you perform vinyl liner replacements?

Yes, we do vinyl liner replacements for inground pools. We provide expert liner replacement services that restore the look and integrity of your pool.

How long does an inground pool installation take?

In our experience, a typical installation can take up to six weeks. We handle every phase of the project from start to finish with precision and care.

Does putting in a pool require a permit?

Yes, and we work closely with local officials to help with that permit process. We guide you through the necessary paperwork and ensure compliance with all local building codes.

Do you do water testing?

Yes, please bring your water sample into our retail showroom located on Route 18 in East Bridgewater.
